# Urban Planning & Smart Cities Data

Geospatial datasets for land use classification, building footprints and 3D city models, population distribution, nighttime light proxies, urban heat islands, noise and air quality, real estate and cadastral data, urban infrastructure networks, mobility and accessibility, and smart city open data platforms. Covers global and country-specific sources relevant to urban planners, municipal analysts, and smart city researchers.

> **Quick Picks**
> - **SOTA**: [Google Dynamic World](https://dynamicworld.app/) -- near real-time (5-day latency) 10m land use/land cover classification globally from Sentinel-2, updated continuously; also on [GEE](https://developers.google.com/earth-engine/datasets/catalog/GOOGLE_DYNAMICWORLD_V1)
> - **Free Best**: [Microsoft Building Footprints](https://github.com/microsoft/GlobalMLBuildingFootprints) -- 1.5B+ global building footprint polygons derived from satellite imagery via AI, free GeoParquet download
> - **Fastest Setup**: [OpenAQ API](https://api.openaq.org/v3/) -- real-time air quality from 30,000+ stations worldwide, no API key required for basic queries
