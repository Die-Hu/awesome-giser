# Space & Aerospace Data

Geospatial data sources covering launch sites, satellite tracking, space weather, airspace management, flight tracking, drone zones, space debris, planetary surfaces, astronomical surveys, and near-Earth objects. This catalog serves aerospace engineers, space situational awareness analysts, aviation GIS specialists, planetary scientists, and anyone working at the intersection of GIS and space.

> **Quick Picks**
> - **SOTA**: [Space-Track.org](https://www.space-track.org) -- the authoritative U.S. DoD catalog of all tracked space objects; TLE/GP data, conjunction messages, decay predictions, login required but free
> - **Free Best**: [CelesTrak](https://celestrak.org) -- Dr. T.S. Kelso's free TLE repository with no login, updated multiple times daily, SOCRATES conjunction tool included
> - **Fastest Setup**: [OpenSky Network REST API](https://opensky-network.org/apidoc/) -- free ADS-B flight state vectors, no key needed for anonymous access, JSON, instant curl queries
